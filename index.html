Glossary of Key Bioinformatics Terms

Gene Regulatory Network (GRN)
A map of how genes and their regulators interact. GRNs show which transcription factors turn genes on or off, revealing the logic of cellular behavior.
Acute Myeloid Leukaemia (AML)
An aggressive blood cancer where immature white blood cells grow uncontrollably. Studying GRNs in AML helps researchers understand how mutations disrupt normal regulation.
Transcription Factor
A protein that binds to DNA and controls whether a gene is expressed. They act like â€œswitchesâ€ in the GRN, activating or repressing target genes.
Chromatin Accessibility
Refers to how open or closed DNA is within the cell nucleus. Open regions are easier for transcription factors to bind, influencing gene expression.

A laboratory technique that identifies where proteins (like transcription factors) bind to DNA across the genome, helping map GRNs.
